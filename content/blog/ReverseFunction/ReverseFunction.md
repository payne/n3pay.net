Jim KX0U talked us through using a radio's reverse function to listen to the input side of a repeater's frequency pair. [On my QB25 radio this is the * on the hand microphone](https://gemini.google.com/share/4df369f9a7f1). One use of the reverse function is to listen and see if another person is close enough to you for simplex to work.
