## My Favorite Features

- **Wireless programming with BLE** - program from a regular computer or cell phone, no cables needed
- **Phone to phone cloning** - easily share radio configurations
- **Dual listening with 101.9 FM** - listen to FM broadcast while monitoring ham frequencies
- **Dual watch of two ham stations** - monitor two frequencies simultaneously
